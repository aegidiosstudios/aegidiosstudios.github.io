Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I do the exercises?
You can do them as often as you like as long as it feels good. But with the longer and more intense exercises, more than once or twice a day is probably over the top.
Can I use my own music?
Yes! You can use your own music, the app's built-in sounds, or practice in silence. When using your own music, it's best to mute the app's retention and relaxation sounds.
How should I breathe when doing the exercises?
You can breathe as you like, but it is generally good to breathe in through the nose and out through the mouth. Try to breathe deeply from your belly and up. Don't force anything.
How can I make a reverberating long hum?
Use your mouth to give the exhale a little resistance and to create a vibration. If you do it right you can feel the vibration from chest to face.
