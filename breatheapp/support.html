Getting Started

Aegidios Breathe is a simple, focused breathing app designed to help you practice various breathing techniques. The app works completely offline and requires no setup.

⚠️ Safety Note:

Only practice breathing exercises when it is safe and you don't have to pay attention to anything else. Sit or lie down. Consult a doctor if you are uncertain of your limitations.

Common Questions

How do I pause a session?

Tap the pause button during any session. You can resume when ready.

Can I customize the sounds?

Yes, in Settings you can enable or disable different sounds individually.

How do I create a custom breathing pattern?

Select "Custom" from the breathing patterns and adjust all parameters in Settings to create your perfect practice.

Troubleshooting

If you experience any issues:

  1. Ensure your device's sound is not muted if using audio features
  2. Try closing and reopening the app
  3. Check if your device needs an update
  4. If issues persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app
