Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pause a session?
You can pause at any time by tapping the pause button. This is especially useful when you want to extend holds or take a break.
Can I use my own music?
Yes! You can use your own music, the app's built-in sounds, or practice in silence. When using your own music, you'll still hear the gong sounds marking important moments in your practice.
What happens after completing the rounds?
There's a relaxation phase that continues until you choose to stop. Take as much time as you need to fully relax and integrate your practice.
Can I customize the exercises?
Yes! While we provide carefully designed preset patterns, you can also create your own custom exercises with full control over all parameters.
