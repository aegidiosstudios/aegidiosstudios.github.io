Privacy Policy

Overview

At Aegidios Studios™, we belive privacy is your right and a necessity for peace of mind. This app is designed to be completely offline and respect your privacy, because that's how we like our apps.

Your Privacy Guarantee

  • ✓ No data collection or tracking of any kind
  • ✓ No internet connection required - works completely offline
  • ✓ All settings stored locally on your device only
  • ✓ No third-party analytics or services
  • ✓ No user accounts or profiles needed

A Personal Note on Privacy

Too many companies harvest our data and profit from it - it's neither fair nor good for us. We're constantly bombarded with messages and notifications based on data collected about us. I don't want any part of that.

This is especially important for an app like Aegidios Breathe, which is meant to be a tool for your wellbeing and peace. That's why you won't find any integration to health apps or community features and no personal profiles, progress logging, badges or stars. This is a distraction free place where you can be you without worrying about your performance or others and what they might be up to.

External Connections

The only connections to the outside world in the app are a few carefully chosen links:

  • This website for information and support
  • Our Instagram for updates and messaging
  • Email button for questions and support
  • Curated music playlists for your practice

That's it. No tracking, no data collection, no distractions - just you and your breath.
