Commercial Policy

Pay Once, Use Forever*

This is a pay once, use forever app. No subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases.

Our Promise to You

  • ✓ No subscription fees ever - we don't believe in endless payments
  • ✓ No gated content - everything is included in your purchase
  • ✓ No ads or data collection - the app price is our only revenue
  • ✓ Access to all updates of the current features

Pricing Strategy

The app is currently priced at $1.99 USD. This introductory price will increase over time as we gather more users and reviews, but price changes will never affect existing users - you keep the price you bought it at.

*While we can't promise 'forever' in absolute terms, we are committed to keeping the app functional and updated for its current feature set for as long as technically possible.

A Personal Note

I built this app because I wanted one and thought what was out there was either inadequate or overpriced. While I would have happily paid $30 for an app like this myself, I understand that without reviews and a established user base, that's a lot to ask.

That's why we're starting at a lower price point. As more users discover and validate the app's value, the price will gradually increase for new users - but never for existing ones.

If we add entirely new features in the future (like guided meditations), these might be offered as optional purchases. But the breathing exercises you bought the app for will always be yours to use in their most up-to-date version.
